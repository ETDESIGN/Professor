# 01. App Overview & Vision

## Project Mission
To transform traditional ESL (English as a Second Language) education by bridging the gap between analog textbooks and digital interaction. The app uses AI as a "Digital Lesson Orchestrator" to convert static content into a premium, gamified, and interactive learning experience.

## Core Value Proposition
- **For Teachers:** Drastically reduces lesson preparation time by automatically digitizing textbooks and generating full-lesson flows (slides, games, assessments).
- **For Students:** Provides a highly engaging, "Duolingo-like" experience that syncs with their classroom material.
- **For Parents:** Offers clear visibility into student progress and shareable milestones (like AI-generated dubbing videos).

## High-Level Goals
1. **Automation:** Turn a photo of a textbook page into a ready-to-play lesson in seconds.
2. **Engagement:** Use gamification (leaderboards, avatars, interactive games) to keep students motivated.
3. **Connectivity:** Create a "Pre-In-Post" learning loop that connects pre-class prep, in-class participation, and post-class reinforcement.
4. **Pedagogical Alignment:** Ensure the digital content remains strictly aligned with the school's chosen physical curriculum.

## Key Ecosystems
- **Lesson Studio (Teacher Prep):** Where the "Magic Scanner" lives.
- **Live Commander (Teacher Remote):** A mobile-first remote control for the classroom.
- **Classroom Board (Digital Stage):** The visual anchor for live lessons.
- **Student Home App (PWA):** The gamified homework and review platform.
- **Parent Portal:** The progress and reporting dashboard.

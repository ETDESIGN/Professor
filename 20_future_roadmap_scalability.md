# 20. Future Roadmap & Scalability

## 1. Goal
To evolve from a textbook companion into a global educational infrastructure.

## 2. Short-Term Roadmap (v2.0)
- **Multi-Player Home Games:** Real-time matches between students at home.
- **In-App Teacher Marketplace:** Teachers can sell their "Approved Blueprints" to other educators.
- **Advanced Phonics AI:** Deeper integration with speech models for nuance (e.g., intonation and stress).

## 3. Mid-Term Roadmap (v3.0)
- **AI Tutors:** Virtual avatars that students can "Chat" with at home to practice conversational English.
- **AR (Augmented Reality) Lessons:** Scanning the classroom floor to spawn virtual "Zoo Animals" for a scavenger hunt.
- **Automatic Translation:** Dynamic translation for non-English speaking parents to understand their child's progress.

---

## 4. Scalability Strategy
- **Infrastructure:** leveraging Supabase's multi-region support to ensure low latency globally.
- **Asset CDN:** Using global edge caching for heavy media files (Videos/Images).
- **Vector Database:** Scaling the "Global Brain" (RAG) to handle millions of textbook fingerprints from different publishers worldwide.

## 5. Monetization Pathways (Optional)
- **Freemium:** Core scanning and games are free; AI-generation and custom avatars are premium.
- **B2B School Licenses:** Enterprise dashboard for school principals to track performance across entire departments.
- **Home Booklet Subscription:** Premium homework content for parents.

# 11. Student App (Home Missions)

## 1. Goal
A PWA designed primarily for mobile use. It acts as the "Duolingo" extension of the classroom material, facilitating the **Pre-class** and **Post-class** phases of the loop.

## 2. Core Experience: The Adventure Map
- **Vertical Path:** A scrolling journey representing the school year.
- **Unit Nodes:** Each node is a unit scanned by the teacher.
    - **Greyed Nodes:** Locked (Not yet published by teacher).
    - **Active Nodes:** Current material.
    - **Cracked Nodes:** Old units requiring "Review" (Spaced Repetition logic).

## 3. Mission Types (Exercises)

### Pre-Class: Preview Mission
- **Goal:** Passive exposure.
- **Activity:** "Listen and see." Swipe through flashcards that will be used in class later. No pressure, just familiarity building.

### Post-Class: The Grind
- **Sentence Scramble:** Drag words to reform sentences from the class story.
- **Listen & Tap:** Hear a word, tap the correct AI-generated image.
- **Speaking Challenge:** Use the phone mic to repeat a target sentence. AI (Gemini) grades pronunciation (1-3 stars).

### The Star Feature: Story Dubbing
- **Activity:** The student records their voice over a comic strip character.
- **Result:** System renders a video with the student's voice.
- **Motivation:** Highly shareable for parents to social media (WeChat/WhatsApp style).

---

## 4. Gamification Loop
- **The Streak (🔥):** Daily login incentive.
- **Hearts (❤️):** 5 lives per day (lost on wrong answers, regenerates over time).
- **XP & Level:** Points feed into the **Classroom Leaderboard**.
- **The Shop:** Spend "Star Coins" on avatar accessories.

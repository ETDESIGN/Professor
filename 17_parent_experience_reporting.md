# 17. Parent Dashboard & Reporting

## 1. Goal
To keep parents engaged and informed, transforming student homework into visible, celebrated achievements.

## 2. Key Features

### The Daily Digest
- **Notification:** "Today in English class, [Student] learned about 'The Farm'!"
- **Summary:** List of vocabulary mastered today.
- **Social Proof:** Shows the student's current position on the class leaderboard.

### The Media Gallery
- **Dubbing Videos:** Parents can watch and download the "Dubbing Missions" their child recorded.
- **Share Button:** Optimized for sharing to social apps (WeChat/WhatsApp/Instagram).
- **Audio Clips:** Listen to pronunciation progress over time.

### Progress Tracking
- **Heatmap:** Similar to GitHub, showing days of consistent practice (The Streak).
- **Skill Radar:** Visualizing proficiency in Listening vs. Speaking vs. Grammar.

---

## 3. Frictionless Access
- **Magic Link Login:** No complex passwords. Parents receive a secure link via email/app invite.
- **Multi-Child View:** One account can switch between multiple siblings' dashboards.

## 4. Teacher-to-Parent Communication
- **AI-Generated Reports:** Instead of the teacher writing reports, Gemini summarizes the week's data into a friendly "Note from the Teacher."
- **Manual Overrides:** Teachers can add personal comments to highlight exceptional effort.

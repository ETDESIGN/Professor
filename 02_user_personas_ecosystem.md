# 02. User Personas & Ecosystem

## 1. User Personas

### Teacher (The Curator & Orchestrator)
- **Pain Points:** High preparation time for interactive lessons, difficult student engagement at home, messy classroom flow switching between tools.
- **Goals:** Scan a book and have a 45-minute lesson ready in seconds. Control the classroom from a phone while staying mobile. Track every student's progress automatically.
- **Devices:** Desktop (Prep), Phone/Tablet (Remote/Scanner).

### Student (The Active Learner)
- **Age Range:** 3-15 years old (ESL Learners).
- **Motivation:** Winning class points (Wheel of Destiny), climbing the leaderboard, customizing their personal avatar.
- **Experience:** No phones in class. PWA access at home for gamified missions.
- **Devices:** Smartphone/Tablet (Home).

### Parent (The Supportive Coach)
- **Goals:** See what their child learned today, share progress/milestones (Videos), and ensure homework is completed.
- **Experience:** Receives push notifications and summaries. Simplified login via link.
- **Devices:** Smartphone.

## 2. The Learning Ecosystem: The "Pre-In-Post" Loop

The app operates on a continuous feedback loop that connects three distinct environments:

### Phase 1: PRE-Class (Flipped Classroom)
- **Trigger:** Teacher publishes a unit 2-3 days before the lesson.
- **Student App:** Unlocks a "Preview Mission."
- **Activity:** Students listen to vocabulary and see images from the upcoming lesson.
- **Goal:** Early exposure to reduce cognitive load during the live class.

### Phase 2: IN-Class (Interaction & Input)
- **Environment:** Classroom Board (Digital Stage) + Teacher Remote.
- **Activities:** Warm-ups (Songs), Vocabulary Input (Flashcards/Hotspots), Story/Comic reading, and Gamified Practice (Team Battles/Wheel).
- **Gamification:** Points earned in real-time sync to the student's global account.

### Phase 3: POST-Class (Reinforcement & Output)
- **Environment:** Student Home App.
- **Activities:** Duolingo-style drills, Spaced Repetition, and "Story Dubbing" (Roleplay recording).
- **Outcome:** Submissions are AI-graded (Gemini) and sent to parents for review and sharing.

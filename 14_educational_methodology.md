# 14. Educational Methodology (ALO7 & Duolingo)

## 1. Goal
To combine the structured, curriculum-based approach of **ALO7** with the high-retention, addictive gamification of **Duolingo**.

## 2. In-Class Inspiration: ALO7
- **The "Pre-In-Post" Loop:** Ensures students see the material three times in different contexts.
- **Interactive Slides:** Using a digital board as the primary teaching tool.
- **Template-Based Learning:** Instead of unique games for every unit, we use high-quality, reusable game templates.
- **Small-Group Mechanics:** Splitting the class into teams for healthy competition.

## 3. Home Inspiration: Duolingo
- **The "Stepping Stone" Map:** A visual sense of progress and accomplishment.
- **The Streak (🔥):** Using psychological triggers to ensure daily practice.
- **Hearts (❤️):** Creating a "Risk vs Reward" scenario to encourage careful attention.
- **Immediate Feedback:** Instant visual/audio cues for every interaction.
- **Spaced Repetition:** The "Cracked Node" system that prompts review of old material.

---

## 4. Adaptations for this App
- **Teacher-Driven Map:** Unlike Duolingo, the map is not static. It is "grown" as the teacher scans and publishes new textbook units.
- **No-Phone Classroom:** High-engagement digital learning without distracting from teacher-led instruction.
- **Direct Alignment:** Every game and mission is 100% derived from the physical textbook the student is using in school.

## 5. Pedagogical Outcomes
- **Vocabulary Retention:** Through visual association and spaced repetition.
- **Grammar Intuition:** Through "Sandbox" mechanics (Drag & Drop) rather than rote memorization of rules.
- **Speaking Fluency:** Through the pressure-free "Story Dubbing" missions at home.
